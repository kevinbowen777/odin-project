New User Signup

Usernhame must be alphanumeric and contain 5 - 12 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g. me@mydomain.com

Password must be alphanumeric (@._ and - are also allowed) and be 8 - 20 characters.

Telephone must be a valid US telephone number (10 digits)

Slug must contain only lowecase letters, numbers and hyphens and be 8 - 20 characters.
